ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Basics of Bodybuilding

My name is Luka Milanovic, I'm a 23 years old and I'm born in Serbia, however I have moved to Sweden in 2012.

I'm a front-end developer but aside from that an amateur bodybuilder as well. So I decided to create BasicsOfBodybuilding that I believe is a decent guide to those who are getting into bodybuilding and to those who want to maybe learn a thing or two and improve their bodybuilding knowledge. There is also a calorie calculator that will help you keep a track on how much you should eat. I have been working out for about 4 years now, and I would say I have a decent basic knowledge for this particular subject.

The informations that this website contains are gathered through various books and internet pages which I will list down.

  • Encyclopedia Of Modern Bodybuilding by Schwarzenegger Arnold
  • Heavy Duty by Mentzer Mike
  • Bodybuilding.com
  • Wikipedia
